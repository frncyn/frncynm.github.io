Nintendo DS Lite Coral Pink

Nintendo DS Lite Coral Pink is a beautifully designed, sleek, and lighter revision of the original Nintendo DS.

Released in North America in 2006, the DS Lite features a brighter screen and a more compact form factor, making it the perfect portable gaming companion. It plays both Nintendo DS and Game Boy Advance (GBA) cartridges.

  • Brighter, adjustable screens
  • Longer battery life
  • Supports DS and GBA games
